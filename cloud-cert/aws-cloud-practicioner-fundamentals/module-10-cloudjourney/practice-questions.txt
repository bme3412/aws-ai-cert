# AWS Module 10: The Cloud Journey Practice Questions (Multiple Choice)

This document contains multiple-choice practice questions based on the AWS Cloud Journey module. The Answer Key is located at the bottom of the file.

---

### Section 1: The AWS Well-Architected Framework

**1. Which pillar of the AWS Well-Architected Framework focuses on the ability of a system to recover from disruptions and dynamically acquire resources to meet demand?**
    a) Operational Excellence
    b) Security
    c) Reliability
    d) Performance Efficiency

**2. A company wants to ensure they are using the most appropriately sized and cost-effective EC2 instances for their workloads. Which pillar of the Well-Architected Framework does this directly support?**
    a) Reliability
    b) Cost Optimization
    c) Performance Efficiency
    d) Sustainability

**3. The principle of protecting systems and data through methods like encryption and managing permissions is central to which Well-Architected Framework pillar?**
    a) Security
    b) Operational Excellence
    c) Cost Optimization
    d) Reliability

**4. The ability to run and monitor systems to deliver business value and to continually improve supporting processes is part of which pillar?**
    a) Performance Efficiency
    b) Sustainability
    c) Operational Excellence
    d) Security

**5. Which pillar focuses on minimizing the environmental impacts of running cloud workloads by reducing energy consumption and increasing efficiency?**
    a) Cost Optimization
    b) Performance Efficiency
    c) Sustainability
    d) Reliability

### Section 2: Benefits of the AWS Cloud

**6. Which benefit of the AWS Cloud eliminates the need for large upfront hardware purchases and allows businesses to pay only for the resources they use?**
    a) Go global in minutes
    b) Stop guessing capacity
    c) Trade upfront expense for variable expense
    d) Benefit from massive economies of scale

**7. By aggregating usage from hundreds of thousands of customers, AWS can offer lower pay-as-you-go prices. What is this benefit called?**
    a) Increase speed and agility
    b) Benefit from massive economies of scale
    c) Go global in minutes
    d) Stop spending money on data centers

**8. What is the primary advantage of the cloud's ability to "Stop guessing capacity"?**
    a) It allows you to deploy applications globally in minutes.
    b) It prevents over-provisioning or under-provisioning of resources.
    c) It lets you focus on your business, not on running data centers.
    d) It reduces the total cost of ownership through volume discounts.

**9. The ability to quickly spin up test environments and innovate without the high cost of hardware investment is an example of which cloud benefit?**
    a) Increase speed and agility
    b) Go global in minutes
    c) Benefit from massive economies of scale
    d) Trade upfront expense for variable expense

**10. Deploying your application to multiple AWS Regions around the world is an example of which key cloud benefit?**
    a) Stop guessing capacity
    b) Stop spending money running and maintaining data centers
    c) Benefit from massive economies of scale
    d) Go global in minutes

---
---

# Answer Key

1.  **c)** Reliability
2.  **b)** Cost Optimization
3.  **a)** Security
4.  **c)** Operational Excellence
5.  **c)** Sustainability
6.  **c)** Trade upfront expense for variable expense
7.  **b)** Benefit from massive economies of scale
8.  **b)** It prevents over-provisioning or under-provisioning of resources.
9.  **a)** Increase speed and agility
10. **d)** Go global in minutes
